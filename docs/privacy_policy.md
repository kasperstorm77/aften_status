# Privacy Policy

**Aften Status** ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Data
- All your evening check-in data is stored **locally on your device** using secure local storage
- We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your personal tracking data
- Your entries, field definitions, and settings remain on your device

### Google Drive Sync (Optional)
- If you choose to enable Google Drive sync, your data is backed up to **your own Google Drive account**
- We do not have access to your Google Drive or your synced data
- You can disable sync at any time in the app settings
- Synced data includes: evening status entries, field definitions, and app settings

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers
- Health data (beyond what you voluntarily enter)

## Third-Party Services

### Google Sign-In
- Used solely for authenticating with your Google account for Drive sync
- We only request access to app-specific data in Google Drive
- We do not access your email, contacts, or other Google services

## Data Deletion
- You can delete all local data at any time through the app settings
- Google Drive data can be deleted from your Google Drive account
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page.
